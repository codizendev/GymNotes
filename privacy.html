GymNotes Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

GymNotes is designed as an offline workout tracker.

Data Collection

  • GymNotes does not collect personal data on a server.
  • GymNotes does not require account creation.
  • GymNotes does not include analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

Data Storage

  • Workout data is stored locally on your device.
  • Data remains on your device unless you explicitly export/share backups or PDFs.

Data Sharing

  • GymNotes does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.
  • If you use export/share features, files are shared only through destinations you choose.

Permissions

  • Notification permission is used for workout reminders.
  • File access is used for import/export operations initiated by you.

Children

  • GymNotes is not directed to children under 13.
